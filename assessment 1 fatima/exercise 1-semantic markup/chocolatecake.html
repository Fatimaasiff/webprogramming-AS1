Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist homemade chocolate cake!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Slowly stir in hot water to make the batter smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool before serving.
